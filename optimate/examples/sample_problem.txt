Our company runs a logistics operation with 8 delivery drivers and 12 daily routes.
Each driver can only cover one route per day due to shift constraints.
We have historical data on how long each driver takes to complete each route,
and we want to minimize total delivery time across all assigned routes.

Some routes require specialized vehicle types (refrigerated trucks), and only
3 of our drivers are certified to operate those vehicles. Exactly 4 of the 12
routes require refrigerated transport.

We also have budget constraints: driver overtime (routes taking more than 8 hours)
costs 1.5x the standard rate. We want to avoid overtime if possible.

Can you identify the optimal assignment of drivers to routes for tomorrow's operations?
